# en-frequency.txt

Top ~28,700 English words (letters only, deduplicated), ranked by real-world
frequency — trimmed from the `en_50k.txt` list in
[hermitdave/FrequencyWords](https://github.com/hermitdave/FrequencyWords)
(2018 OpenSubtitles-derived word frequencies), MIT licensed.

Used by `src/lib/word-resplit.ts` to re-segment PDF text where extraction
glued words together with no spaces (see DEV_LOG.md).
